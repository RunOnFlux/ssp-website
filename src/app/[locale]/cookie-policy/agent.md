Cookie Policy for the sspwallet.io / sspwallet.com website (this policy
covers only the website — the browser extension and mobile app have
separate Privacy Policy and Terms of Service coverage). Issued by InFlux
Technologies Limited (UK).

## What this page covers

- Three cookie categories: Strictly Necessary (always on), Functional
  (theme, language, display), Analytics (Google Analytics — opt-in only).
- Privacy-first defaults: Google Analytics is disabled until the user
  accepts analytics cookies via the consent banner.
- Browser-level cookie controls (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
- GDPR compliance: explicit consent for non-essential cookies, withdrawal
  rights, granular controls.
- Data retention: session cookies cleared on close; analytics data up to
  26 months per Google's defaults.

## How to access cookie settings

- On first visit, the cookie consent banner lets you Accept All, Accept
  Only Necessary, or Customize.
- To change your choice later: click the "Cookie Settings" button in the
  website footer (also surfaced from /contact's footer area).
- To re-trigger the banner: clear browser cookies for sspwallet.io and
  reload.
- Browser-level: use your browser's privacy / cookie settings to block
  cookies entirely (may break functionality).

## Where to ask Cookie Policy questions

- Email info@sspwallet.io with subject "Cookie Policy Inquiry".
