# SSP Academy

The SSP Academy is the educational arm of SSP Wallet. Articles are categorized by topic (multisig, security, getting started, how-to, coin guides, DeFi, news explained) and may belong to a multi-part series.

## Sub-routes

- `/academy/{category}` — category landing page
- `/academy/{category}/{slug}` — individual article
- `/academy/series/{slug}` — multi-part learning path

## Categories

- multisig — How 2-of-2 multisignature actually works in SSP
- getting-started — Foundational concepts every crypto user should know
- security — Protect your crypto: seeds, phishing, hardware, threat models
- how-to — Step-by-step walkthroughs for common SSP tasks
- coin-guides — Deep dives into individual coins and chains SSP supports
- defi — Staking, lending, swaps, and ERC-4337 explained
- news-explained — Context and analysis on crypto news and regulation
