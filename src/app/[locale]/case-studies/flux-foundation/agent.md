How the Flux Foundation uses SSP Enterprise to secure the Fusion bridge and the
Foundation treasury — replacing scattered multisig wallets and custom scripts
with a single self-custody platform.

## Key facts

- Use case: Foundation treasury + Fusion bridge multisig.
- Vault model: M-of-N multisig with two devices required per signer (browser
  extension + mobile app).
- Chains in production: BTC, ETH, FLUX, EVM L2s — same platform across UTXO and
  EVM.
- Replaces internal tooling (custom scripts, chat-thread audit trail) with an
  attributable on-platform record of every proposal, approval, and rejection.
- True self-custody: no MPC vendor key share, no custodian — funds remain
  recoverable from signer seeds even if SSP went away.
- Status: in production. Quotes attributed to Tadeas Kmenta, Flux Foundation.
