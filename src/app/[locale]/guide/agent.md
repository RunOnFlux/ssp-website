Complete two-part installation guide for SSP Wallet (browser extension) plus
SSP Key (mobile 2FA app). Both devices are required for the 2-of-2
multisignature system.

## Key facts

- Setup takes ~15 minutes end-to-end across two devices.
- Part One (browser extension): Chrome, Brave, or Firefox. 7 steps including
  password creation and seed-phrase backup.
- Part Two (mobile): SSP Key on iOS 15.1+ or Android 7+. 7 steps including
  separate Key seed-phrase backup and QR-code sync with the wallet.
- Two seed phrases — wallet seed and key seed — must be backed up
  independently. Both are needed to recover the wallet.
- Embedded video walkthrough (3:36) at /ssp-setup-guide.webm with .mp4
  fallback and English captions track. Video poster at
  /ssp-setup-guide-poster.jpg.
- Page emits HowTo, VideoObject, and BreadcrumbList JSON-LD.
